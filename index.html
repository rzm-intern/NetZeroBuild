What is Green Building Certification?

Green building certification provides third-party assurance that projects meet critical sustainability standards. Vision 2030 has made this a national priority, creating a significant market opportunity.

The Catalyst: Vision 2030

Green building certification is now essential for real estate worldwide, ensuring sustainability, efficiency, and long-term asset value. In Saudi Arabia, Vision 2030 and the Saudi Green Initiative make certification a regulatory requirement across giga-projects and public developments. Mostadam, the Kingdom’s national framework, anchors this shift, creating surging demand for efficient compliance solutions as projects like NEOM, Diriyah, and Roshn embed green standards into every asset.

The Cost Burden

Manual certification adds $60,000 to over $700,000 per project in fees and consultant hours. Net Zero Build's platform directly targets these inefficiencies, unlocking significant cost savings for clients.

Giga-Projects Use-Case: NEOM PoC

Embed the AI core into the client’s sustainability procedures and plan of work to automate multi-standard certification (e.g., LEED/WELL/Envision) across masterplans, infrastructure, and buildings, with live compliance tracking and automated documentation checks.

Deliver portfolio dashboards and real-time validation to streamline governance and green-finance/investor reporting across business units and projects.

Impact: ~60% admin-effort reduction and ~40% certification-related CapEx reduction; ~$200k–$400k savings per certification and ~$60–$120M across ~300 certifications over three years.

Commercials: 12-month PoC budget SAR 4M, with a pathway to multi-year rollout upon successful pilot.
